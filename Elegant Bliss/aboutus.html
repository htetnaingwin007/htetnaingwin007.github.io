Our Story

The Wedding Planners Monaco (WPM) was founded by Imène HANAFI in 2015, and since then, she and her team based in Monaco carry on sharing The Wedding Planners Monaco’s unique vision of glamorous events and refined lifestyle experiences. Imène has always challenged herself in order to create unique experiences for the brides and grooms that choose her as their wedding planner for their event on the French Riviera or in Italy. The key to her success: Putting the client’s story at the heart of any event and delivering high-end wedding celebrations designed to produce memories that last. In addition to this, a commitment to hard work and personalization allows the WPM’s team to always reach excellence expected by its refined clientele.
