Dolce Vita Brunch at Chateau Saint-Jeannet – French Riviera (S & A’s wedding)

June 16th 2024 - 200 guests

Our wonderful couple S & A, from Dubai, fell in love with my elevated, luxurious designs and tasked me to create something truly unique for their pre-wedding brunch. I imagined transporting them and their guests to a Dolce & Gabbana meets Dior setting on the Amalfi Coast, creating the feeling of a multi-destination wedding weekend right in the heart of the French Riviera. The Dolce Vita-themed brunch was a celebration of elegance and Mediterranean charm, with the exclusive Château Saint-Jeannet, perched on a hilltop with sweeping views of the Côte d’Azur, as the perfect backdrop. This prestigious wedding venue in the South of France blended rustic charm with refined sophistication, exactly as S & A envisioned.

Photos by Dmitry Garvanko Photography

Video by AG Studio Videography

Mediterranean Elegance in Every Detail

Imène Hanafi, a renowned wedding planner who specialises in exclusive events across Monaco, the French Riviera South of France and Italy, brought her signature touch of indulgence and opulence to every detail. This wasn’t just a brunch—it was an experience that transported guests to the heart of Southern Italy, all while basking in the beauty of the French Riviera.

Drawing from Southern Italy’s vibrant coastal aesthetic, the décor for S & A’s Dolce Vita wedding brunch was nothing short of spectacular. The tablescapes were a true highlight, fusing Mediterranean elegance with fine craftsmanship. Lush white roses, hydrangeas, and cascading orchids paired beautifully with bright lemons, creating a vibrant nod to the Amalfi Coast’s sunny appeal.

Ornate Sicilian Moorish head vases, each a piece of art, adorned the centre of every table alongside delicate ceramic busts, lending a sense of heritage to the décor. The blue-and-white patterned table linens evoked coastal charm, while wicker placemats, finely detailed plates, and vibrant blue glassware completed the sophisticated Italian look. The entire design was a harmonious blend of Mediterranean colour and elegance, inviting guests to indulge in a beautifully curated setting on the French Riviera.

A Culinary Journey Through Southern Italy

While the décor set the scene, the culinary experience was just as captivating. Imène ensured that guests were treated to a sumptuous feast, with a menu inspired by the rich flavours of Southern Italy. The brunch was served under a charming awning dressed in real lemons,where guests savoured a range of dishes, from freshly baked focaccia and pizzas to delicate lemon and pistachio cakes, each dish paying homage to Italy’s celebrated food culture.

The dessert table was a particular highlight, featuring elegant cakes and repurposed Sicilian bust vases filled with fresh herbs like basil and tomatoes—combining culinary flair with creative design. Every element was carefully curated to provide a luxurious yet relaxed experience, embodying the laid-back refinement that defines both the French Riviera and Southern Italy.

The Ultimate in Exclusive Wedding Planning

S & A’s wedding brunch was a true masterpiece—an exclusive and serene celebration that transported guests to a Mediterranean paradise, all while enjoying the prestige and beauty of Château Saint-Jeannet. For those seeking an unforgettable wedding in the South of France, this historic Château is the perfect choice, offering secluded elegance and awe-inspiring views.

With Imène’s expertise and The Wedding Planners Monaco’s renowned services, couples can expect bespoke weddings and events that go beyond expectations. Whether you’re dreaming of a luxury wedding overlooking the Côte d’Azur or an intimate celebration in a château, Imène and her team bring creativity, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of high-end wedding design to every event.
