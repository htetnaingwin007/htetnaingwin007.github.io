A consulting service

We are more than a service provider, we are a right-hand man in the organization of your wedding. We will help you identify your needs and support you in making decisions throughout the preparations. We are attentive and here to reassure you, especially when you have doubts. We are here to help you think about the details you would not have thought of. As professionals, we set up reporting tools for better monitoring of wedding preparations.

Valentin ERIAUD-DUPIN

Agency Director - Wedding & Event Planner

Wedding Planner trained at the EFMM, Valentin ERIAUD-DUPIN is the director of the V&F WEDDING AGENCY agency. After starting his career in finance, Valentin wanted to turn to a more human profession and return to one of his first passions: events. With his personal and professional experiences, Valentin is a conductor who will be able to support you and advise you in the organization of your high-end event.

Increased availability

We are here to reassure you and we are committed to responding to you as soon as possible through the communication channel of your choice within a short period of time. If it is to reassure you, we will respond to you very quickly by text message.

Team presence on the day

We do not design a wedding without a minimum of two people on the big day to coordinate the whole day. A team of at least two people allows us to better control the flow of service providers. We can be more numerous depending on the organizational options chosen and the missions entrusted to the Agency.

Our network

Trained at EFMM, the leading training school for wedding professions, we have also been a member of ASSOCEM since 2024, a national association bringing together Wedding Planners, Wedding Designers and Wedding Celebrants. We adhere to the professional code of ethics established by the association for the respect of our clients and our colleagues.
