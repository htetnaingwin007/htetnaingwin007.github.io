With over twenty years of collective experience in planning weddings located anywhere between Monaco and Saint-Tropez, from lake of Como to the Amalfi coast in Italy, The Wedding Planners Monaco have decided to take wedding planning to another level.
We can organize the wedding of your dreams in any venue on the French Riviera and Italy: in a medieval French chateau, a luxurious hotel ballroom, a legendary villa in Italy, outdoors al fresco, on a super yacht, a charming beach location lit up with fairy lights or even in your own villa!
