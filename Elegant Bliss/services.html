AN EXCLUSIVE EXPERIENCE

IN THE COUNTRY OF LOVE

With us, quality takes precedence over quantity.
This is why we engage in a limited number of weddings per year.
And we would love for yours to be one of them.

By actively listening to your wishes and ideas, we will uncover the mood and aesthetic you are dreaming of. Then we will thoughtfully align you with the right creative partners to bring it all to life.

A well-executed event involves forethought and preparation. So, from the start, we will look at your event from every imaginable angle. And create a plan A, B, and C — along with creative solutions for whatever comes our way.

As your wedding weekend approaches, we will orchestrate and manage everything while serving and honoring you and your experience. So you and your loved ones can relax and savor this monumental event — without distraction or worry.

Forever is composed of now

- Emilie Dickinson -

FEATURED SERVICES

+ VENUE SELECTION Extensive knowledge of countryside villas, French chateaus, iconic mansions, and luxury hotels. Plus vital information for you to consider as you determine the perfect venue for you.
+ DESIGN CONCEPTS
A collection of images and design elements to help you visualize and decide on the look and feel of your day.
+ SCOUTING TRIP
The perfect chance for us to meet and connect in person. And an essential opportunity for us to tour venues, meet with vendors, and begin visualizing how everything will come together.
+ VENDOR REFERRALS & COMMUNICATION
Based on your budget and style, we will thoughtfully align you with the right creative partners. Then, collaboratively work alongside them to bring everything to life.​​​​​​​ ​​​​​​​

+ FINANCIAL ESTIMATE A detailed plan that clearly outlines your choices and costs. So you can gauge your options and track your decisions with confidence and trust.
+ PROJECT MANAGEMENT
Clear and comprehensive online planning tools, customized to you and your needs, and accessible 24-7. So you never have to wait or wonder where things stand.
+ BI-WEEKLY PLANNING CALLS
Regularly scheduled check-in calls to address your questions, keep everyone on track, and help bridge the distance. As we embrace long-distance planning —together.
+ VENDOR COMMUNICATION
Frequent contact with each of your vendor partners to manage your expectations and ensure everyone is informed, prepared, and ready to deliver.

+ WEDDING WEEKEND SCHEDULE
A thorough yet flexible agenda to ensure everyone, from destination guests to vendors, understands the broader context of the plan. And their place within it.
+ ONSITE SUPPORT
A well-balanced team of planners to oversee and navigate your day with grace. And ensure a seamless and enjoyable atmosphere for everyone involved.
+ MULTI-DAY EVENT MANAGEMENT
Direction and support for all of your wedding celebrations, so you and your guests have more time to immerse yourselves in the beauty of France and the joy of your festivities.
+ GUEST EXPERIENCE
Stellar service, welcoming hospitality, and a well-thought-out guest experience that enhances the overall quality and meaningfulness of your celebration.
